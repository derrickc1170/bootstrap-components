In 1998, the Culling Incident took place at the ARK Facility when Subject 16892, a highly experimental entity, became violent and killed all the staff.The subject had been showing strange behavior during experiments, and one day, it broke free from containment and attacked.It used weapons from the facility to kill everyone in its path. The facility was abandoned, and Subject 16892 was never found.The incident was never spoken about again due to the tragic event.
